ⓘ Info:

Hong Kong is a bustling city-state with a population of over 7 million people. It is a leading financial center and hub for international trade and commerce. The city offers a unique blend of Eastern and Western cultures, a diverse food scene, and many world-class attractions, such as Victoria Harbour and the Peak Tram. Hong Kong is a popular destination for tourists and expats alike, with a cosmopolitan vibe and dynamic atmosphere.
